Our Profile

Crafted for connoisseurs. Created to be remembered.

At Fruclate, we redefine indulgence through the art of chocolate-making. From our home in Bandung, West Java, we bring together the richness of carefully selected cocoa and the vibrant essence of fresh, handpicked fruit—curating flavors that feel refined, modern, and unmistakably memorable.

Every piece we craft carries our signature: disciplined technique, elevated taste, and quiet elegance. We honor time-tested methods while welcoming innovation, ensuring each bite is a harmony of texture, aroma, and depth—made for those who appreciate the finer details.

Beyond taste, Fruclate stands for integrity. We collaborate with local growers, uphold strict quality standards, and design with intention—so that what you hold is not merely chocolate, but an experience: thoughtful, beautiful, and worthy of life’s most meaningful moments.

Fruclate is where craftsmanship meets emotion—an invitation to savor, to share, and to remember.

What Defines Us

  • Premium cocoa and real fruit infusions—balanced with precision.
  • Heritage craft meets modern innovation for timeless flavor.
  • Thoughtful design and packaging—elegant, purposeful, memorable.
  • Responsible sourcing with uncompromising quality standards.
